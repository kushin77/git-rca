# Pilot Recruitment Guide

Purpose
- Provide templates and guidance to recruit 3-5 pilot engineers for the Investigation Canvas validation.

Who to recruit
- Engineers who regularly perform incident investigations or on-call rotations.
- Mix of senior and mid-level engineers to capture diverse feedback.

Recruitment channels
- Slack: #eng-ops, #oncall, and team-specific channels.
- Email: Engineering mailing list and team leads.
- Direct outreach: PMs or Engineering Managers to nominate participants.

Selection criteria
- Willingness to participate for a 30â€“45 minute guided session.
- Available during the pilot window (TBD).
- Able to run through a sample investigation (real or synthetic).

Responsibilities of recruited pilots
- Attend a guided session; complete the survey after the session; optionally provide follow-up details.
- Trial the Investigation Canvas and attempt to link events and annotate findings.
